Biography

Lyric Tenor

A voice forged
in tradition

Argentine-Italian lyric tenor Rolando Guy is based in Berlin, singing opera, concert and recital engagements across Germany and Europe. His work is rooted in the Italian line and Lieder tradition, extending into operetta and Argentine tango when the programme calls for colour and theatrical immediacy.

He collaborates with opera houses, orchestras, festivals and presenters who need a voice that combines musical discipline, languages on the tongue, and a vivid stage presence—from principal roles and gala nights to curated chamber programmes and recital series.

Selected performances, press excerpts, high-resolution photos, and downloadable presenter materials are available across Calendar, Media, and Press & materials.

For presenters
